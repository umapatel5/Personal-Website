About Me

Hi! I’m Uma, a passionate Freelance Web Developer with a love for creating engaging, user-friendly websites. Specializing in front-end development, I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft seamless digital experiences. My journey into the tech world began with a fascination for understanding how things work, which has led me to explore various programming languages and frameworks to expand my technical expertise.

I’m currently a student at NYU and an active member of Tech@NYU, where I collaborate with like-minded peers to tackle innovative projects. Last semester, I built a full-stack website called "LangConv", designed to simplify language learning through real-time conversation simulations. This project allowed me to dive deeper into backend development and strengthen my skills in database management and API integration.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, sketching creative ideas, or working on personal projects that merge technology with creativity. With a growth mindset and a commitment to continuous learning, I’m always seeking opportunities to refine my skills and contribute to impactful projects that make a difference.
