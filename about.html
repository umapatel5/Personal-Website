About Me

Hi! I’m Uma, a passionate Freelance Web Developer with a love for creating engaging and user-friendly websites. I specialize in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My journey into the tech world began with a fascination for how things work, leading me to explore various programming languages and frameworks.

I’m currently a student at NYU, where I actively participate in Tech@NYU. This semester, I am working on a full-stack website project called "Earthly Bound," focused on promoting sustainability and encouraging people to adopt eco-friendly habits. The platform will feature weekly challenges, customizable goals, token rewards, links to informative articles, and a chat function for users to connect and share ideas.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, drawing, or working on personal projects that combine my love for technology and creativity. I believe in continuous learning and strive to improve my skills and contribute to exciting projects that make a difference.
